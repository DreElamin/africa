The Opportunity

200 Million People.
90% Without Access.

A structural funding gap is leaving Africa's largest underserved market without solutions. Across Africa, 200 million people require at least one assistive product — from wheelchairs and prosthetics to communication devices and learning tools.

Yet 90% lack access due to availability, affordability, and suitability. The market isn't competitive — it barely exists.

Who We Are

Africa's AT
Ecosystem
Builder

AT4D (Assistive Technologies for Disability Trust) is a spin-out of the Innovate Now Accelerator, delivered by the Global Disability Innovation Hub through the UK government-funded AT2030 programme. We have built the continent's largest network of AT startups and the infrastructure needed to support them.

Disability-led from the top — our leadership, advisory board, and investment committee include people with lived experience of disability, ensuring every decision is grounded in the realities of the communities we serve.

Our Approach

Ecosystem,
Not Just a Portfolio

Moving beyond writing cheques and into building the conditions for AT ventures to succeed. Our model blends structured investment with hands-on venture building, evidence generation, and market creation.

01
Structured Investment
Flexible instruments — SAFE notes, convertible notes, direct equity, revenue-based financing — matched to venture stage and needs.
02
Venture Building
Tailored technical assistance, product validation, governance strengthening, and go-to-market support.
03
Evidence Generation
Impact measurement, user research, and the Momentous Playbook — establishing the investment case for African AT.
04
Market Building
Research, knowledge dissemination, and thought leadership to expand the investable AT ecosystem.
05
Strategic Partnerships
Distribution partnerships, co-investment opportunities, and ecosystem access for portfolio companies.
06
Governance Support
Board development, financial management, compliance frameworks, and operational best practices.
Validate
Prove the commercial case for African AT through structured investment, real portfolio data, and the Momentous Playbook — replacing anecdote with evidence.
De-risk
Reduce perceived investment risk through governance support, transparent reporting, and hands-on venture building — making AT ventures legible to institutional investors.
Crowd In Capital
Use early results to attract larger pools of impact and commercial capital to African AT — moving from pioneering fund to market catalyst.
